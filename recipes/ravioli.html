This handcrafted lasagna recipe is perfect for your hungry minions.

Ingredients

  • Childrens tears
  • Pasta
  • Sauce
  • Onions
  • Cheese

Directions

  1. Place pasta in foil pan.
  2. Mix sauce tears and onions.
  3. Pour mixture on pasta.
  4. Cover sauce with Cheese.
